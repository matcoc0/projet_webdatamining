Luke Skywalker is a Jedi who was born on Tatooine. His sister Leia Organa is a leader in the Rebellion.
Leia is the daughter of Anakin Skywalker and Padmé Amidala. Anakin became Darth Vader.
Obi-Wan Kenobi trained Anakin and later guided Luke. Yoda was a wise Jedi Master who trained generations.
Han Solo is a smuggler who became a hero of the Rebellion. He pilots the Millennium Falcon with Chewbacca.
Chewbacca is a Wookie from the planet Kashyyyk. The Millennium Falcon is a fast starship.
Darth Vader serves Emperor Palpatine. Palpatine turned Anakin to the dark side of the Force.
Rey is a Jedi from Jakku. She trained with Leia and Luke. Finn was a stormtrooper who joined the Resistance.
Kylo Ren is the son of Han and Leia. He is torn between light and dark. He commands the First Order.
The Death Star was destroyed by the Rebellion. The second Death Star was destroyed at the Battle of Endor.
Lando Calrissian helped Han and Leia escape Cloud City. R2-D2 and C-3PO are loyal droids.
R2-D2 served Padmé, Anakin, and later Luke. C-3PO speaks over six million languages.
Jabba the Hutt was a gangster on Tatooine. Boba Fett captured Han Solo.
The Mandalorian protects Grogu, a young Force-sensitive being. Ahsoka Tano was Anakin’s apprentice.
General Grievous led the droid army during the Clone Wars. Count Dooku trained Grievous and fought Yoda.
